Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide our service:

  • Google account email (for authentication)
  • Nickname, gender, and date of birth
  • Language preferences
  • Voice data (for female host verification only — not stored)
  • Avatar selection
  • Call history and transaction records

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used exclusively for:

  • Matching you with compatible users
  • Processing coin purchases and withdrawals
  • Improving your experience
  • Preventing fraud and abuse

We NEVER sell your data to third parties.

3. Data Storage

All data is stored on Firebase (Google Cloud Platform) and Supabase, with servers located in India (asia-south1). Data is encrypted in transit and at rest.

4. Payments

All payments are processed by PayU. HumDum does not store your UPI ID, banking details, or card information. PayU's privacy policy applies to all transactions.

5. Voice Data

Voice verification audio is processed momentarily and immediately discarded. We do not store voice recordings. Voice data is never used for any purpose other than one-time gender verification.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time

To exercise these rights, email hello@humdum.app. We respond within 7 days.

7. Data Retention

We retain your data as long as your account is active. Upon account deletion, all personally identifiable data is permanently removed within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

HumDum is strictly for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.
