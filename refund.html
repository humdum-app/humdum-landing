Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Coin Purchases

Coins are digital goods delivered instantly upon successful payment. All coin purchases are final and non-refundable, except in the following cases:

  • Duplicate charge for the same transaction
  • Coins not credited within 1 hour of successful payment
  • Technical error on HumDum's side

2. Refund Process

To request a refund, email hello@humdum.app with your transaction ID within 7 days. Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days to the original payment method.

3. Cancellation

Users can delete their account at any time from Settings → Delete Account. Unused coins are forfeited upon account deletion.

4. Chargebacks

Unauthorized chargebacks may result in account suspension. Contact us first to resolve any billing issues.
